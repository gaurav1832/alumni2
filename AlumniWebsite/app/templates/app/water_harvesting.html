Water Scarcity in India

India relies on its agriculture today, and on its industries tomorrow. Water is insufficient for the farmers even today, and it will keep us from ever becoming a developed industrial nation. For the country, its people and every household, its essential to conserve water, and use the best means possible. For if we don't, none of use will have easily obtainable water anymore, many of us already don't.

Why Us?

Our problems are extremely grave, and we need to act now. Un-managed administrative condition are making the world collapse on us. Take a look at the stats, it says so strongly - Act fast, act hard.

Make in India? You just can't.

We talk about entrepreneurship and business ideas, the fact is, you just can't. Industries are best set up where there is water is cheaply available, if we don't start now, it will not be, anywhere in the country. Growth, entrepreneurship, ideas, all that we are here for, only happens if you have water.

The Plan - to act.


Students campaigns achieve great success everywhere in the world, and its time we do it here. Stuff people don't usually identify with, like HelpAge India, Smile, etc, gather huge money, we can certainly get at least a few people to do what they are legally obligated to, and to live a quality life of peace and productivity. Some people do listen, some people will listen, and that's all we need. Not to reach every household, but to get a few hundred for a start, people who listen and understand, and if you can't convince many others, just start with your home.

I ask each of you to attempt to convince parents, family, relatives, neighbours, and everyone you meet to get water harvesting installed, we will help you planning and getting stuff done, and with some help from all of you, we can have these systems in 100s of homes, and save so much water. The installations only cost a couple of thousands, dig holes and start off, and they restore a lot of ground water, that is cheaply available to both people and businesses. Everyone can afford then, everyone should, and everyone identifies with the problems faced when we don't. Apartments have the benefit of needing even less money per person to do this stuff, just a will to act. And if you have it, please don't let a sense of hopelessness pull you down. Volunteer to save the planet.
